S Bar by Katsuya: Brentwood’s Best Kept Nightlife Secret

By Westsider StaffFebruary 20265 min read

Tucked inside the original Katsuya on San Vicente Boulevard in Brentwood, S Bar operates as its own thing entirely — a sultry, high-energy lounge with craft cocktails, a kitchen pulling from one of LA's best Japanese menus, and a bar staff that makes you feel like a regular from the moment you walk in. Most people drive past it on the way to somewhere else. That's their loss.

S Bar sits behind Katsuya's dining room with an indoor-outdoor setup and al fresco seating under the stars. Wednesday through Sunday nights, it fills up with a Westside crowd that knows where to go without being told. Walk-ins only — no reservations — which gives it an energy that booked-up spots never quite manage.

“The bartenders here don’t just make drinks — they make the night. That combination of a serious cocktail program and genuinely welcoming service is rarer than it should be.”

The Cocktails

The drink menu reads like a Hollywood playlist — cocktails named Purple Rain, Sleepless in Seattle, Stairway to Heaven, Sahara Tent. The names are playful but the execution is serious: balanced, well-sourced, made by people who actually know what they're doing. Expect to spend $18–$25 a drink and not feel like you overpaid. The bar is well-stocked and the bartenders move fast on a busy Friday without cutting corners.

What sets S Bar apart from other upscale Westside spots isn't just the drinks — it's the people serving them. The bar staff has a warmth that's genuinely unusual at this price point. You can show up alone, sit at the bar, and leave an hour later feeling like you've been coming here for years.

Happy Hour

Happy hour is when S Bar becomes an outright steal. Discounted cocktails and bar bites — and among those bites, Katsuya's famous Spicy Tuna Crispy Rice. If you've never had it: crispy sushi rice topped with spicy tuna and a sliver of jalapeño. It's one of the best single bites in West LA, and getting it at happy hour prices at a bar is the right way to experience it.

Other standouts from the bar food menu: the Yellowtail Sashimi with Jalapeño, Baked Crab Handrolls, and the full Katsuya dinner menu is available if you want to make a proper night of it.

Best Night to GoThursday is the sweet spot — lively enough to have real energy, not so packed that getting a seat takes work. DJs run Thursday through Saturday. Wednesday is the quietest and best for conversation. Walk-ins only, so arrive before 8pm on weekends if you want to secure a spot at the bar.

The Vibe

S Bar skews upscale without being stiff. The crowd is mixed — industry people, Brentwood locals, people who just finished dinner at Katsuya and didn't want the night to end. The music gets louder as the night progresses. The outdoor patio is the right call on a clear Westside evening, which in Brentwood is most evenings.

Getting There

S Bar is at 11777 San Vicente Blvd, Brentwood — inside Katsuya. Street parking on San Vicente or in the Brentwood Gardens structure nearby. Open Wednesday and Sunday until midnight, Thursday through Saturday until 1am. Closed Monday and Tuesday.
